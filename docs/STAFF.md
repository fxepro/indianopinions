# Staff access — Indian Opinions

## Sign in

1. Open **https://indianopinions.com/sign-in**
2. Enter your staff email and password
3. You are redirected to **https://admin.indianopinions.com/admin**

## Admin dashboard

Bookmark and use:

**https://admin.indianopinions.com/admin**

Do **not** bookmark the Railway URL (`*.up.railway.app`). It is for infrastructure only.

## Public site

The marketing site and sign-in page live on **https://indianopinions.com**.

## Local development

| Service | URL |
|---------|-----|
| Next.js (public + sign-in) | http://localhost:9002 |
| Laravel (API + admin) | http://localhost:8000 |

Default editor login after seeding: `editor@indianopinions.com` / `password`

## Support

If sign-in fails with a session error, hard-refresh the sign-in page or clear cookies for `indianopinions.com` and try again.
